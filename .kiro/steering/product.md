# Spec-Tape Audio Companion

A spooky-themed portable audio app created for the Kiroween hackathon. The app combines AI-powered audio analysis with ghostly effects and digital sharing capabilities.

## Core Features

- **Ghost Writer AI**: Intelligent audio analysis that generates spooky narratives and manifestation reports
- **EVP Analysis**: Real-time Electronic Voice Phenomenon detection in Seance Mode
- **Digital Spirit Shards**: Shareable tokens containing Spectral Mixes that can be transferred between devices
- **Spectral Effects**: Audio processing including Whisper/Hiss Filter and Cryptic Resonator
- **Wireless Connectivity**: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and NFC for sharing and syncing

## Spooky Theme Elements

- Midnight detection triggers special vocabulary
- Bass level detection activates paranormal terminology
- Random easter eggs (1 in 100 chance for special EVP messages)
- "Wooooo" sound effects on successful Spirit Shard transfers
- Cryptic Resonator provides low-frequency echoing reverb

## Target Use Cases

- Audio recording and playback with supernatural effects
- Real-time EVP analysis and tutoring
- Sharing spooky audio experiences through Digital Spirit Shards
- Creating and managing Spectral Mixes with AI-generated narratives
