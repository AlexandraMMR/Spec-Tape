## VISUAL NOTES FOR RECORDING

- **Lighting**: Dim with colored LED strips (purple/green for spooky vibes)
- **Audio**: Clear microphone, add subtle reverb in post for atmosphere
- **Screen Recording**: Use OBS at 1080p, ensure cassette animations are smooth
- **Transitions**: Quick cuts, VHS glitch effects between sections
- **Background Music**: Low-volume synthwave or spooky ambient throughout
- **Text Overlays**: Use retro computer font for key features
  - "Single HTML File"
  - "AI-Powered EVP Analysis"
  - "90s Aesthetic"
  - "Hidden Easter Eggs"

## TIMING BREAKDOWN

| Section | Time | Duration | Focus |
|---------|------|----------|-------|
| Hook | 0:00-0:15 | 15s | Grab attention with nostalgia + spooky twist |
| Pitch | 0:15-0:30 | 15s | What it is, what it does, why it's cool |
| Demo: Mixtape | 0:30-0:55 | 25s | Show core functionality, visual appeal |
| Demo: EVP | 0:55-1:30 | 35s | Highlight unique AI feature, show output |
| Easter Eggs | 1:30-1:45 | 15s | Fun extras, engagement hooks |
| Tech & Close | 1:45-2:00 | 15s | Credibility, thank you, call to action |

## ENERGY LEVELS

- **Hook**: HIGH - Grab attention immediately
- **Pitch**: MEDIUM-HIGH - Enthusiastic but clear
- **Demo**: MEDIUM - Let the product shine, guide smoothly
- **Easter Eggs**: HIGH - Build excitement again
- **Close**: MEDIUM-HIGH - Strong finish, memorable

## KEY PHRASES TO EMPHASIZE

- "Single-file web app" (technical achievement)
- "AI-powered paranormal investigation" (unique value prop)
- "Authentic 90s aesthetic" (design quality)
- "No installation required" (ease of use)
- "Hidden Easter eggs" (engagement factor)

---

**Pro Tips:**
- Practice the script 3-4 times before recording
- Keep energy high but natural—you're excited, not manic
- Smile when talking, it comes through in your voice
- If you mess up, just pause and restart that section
- Record in 30-second chunks if needed, edit together
- Upload to YouTube as "Unlisted" first to test, then make Public
- Mark as "Not for Kids" on YouTube for judge accessibility
